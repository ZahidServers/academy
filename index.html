How Webaon Academy works

  1. Apply & Enroll: Choose your program and submit details. Flexible start dates.
  2. Live classes: Weekly live sessions on MS Teams / Zoom. Students choose their daily commitment.
  3. Projects & Capstone: Multiple projects and a final capstone evaluated by your instructor.
  4. Certificates: Completion, Attendance & Capstone certificates after course completion.
  5. Guaranteed Internship: Remote internships at Webaon / sister / partner companies. Internships are flexible — students may choose role or skip.
  6. Letters & Performance Certs: On internship completion students receive LOR & performance certificate.
Delivery model

Live sessions (MS Teams / Zoom), recordings available, 1:1 instructor capstone review, GitHub project portfolio for every student.

Stack common to Web Dev courses

MySQL, MongoDB, Postman, Docker, HTML/CSS/SCSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap 5, Three.js (intro), Anime.js, Git & GitHub.

Internship Pathway

Guaranteed remote internships

Placements at Webaon, subsidiaries, sister companies or partner firms. Roles may vary — we prioritize alignment with student skills but offer flexibility.

  • Remote & flexible schedules
  • Multiple roles available (dev, QA, infra, marketing tech)
  • Students can choose or skip internship; eligibility T&Cs apply
Certificates on completion

Course Completion, Attendance, Capstone Certificate. If internship completed: Internship Completion Certificate, Letter of Recommendation & Performance Certificate.
